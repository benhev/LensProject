Model Lens_CNN_v1
90000 instances of 100x100 images with 1 color channels validated against 10000 test samples.
Batch size: 20
Number of Epochs: 10
Loss Function: mean_squared_error
Model Architecture: An input layer + 3 hidden convolution layers with kernel_size=(3, 3) and 16 filters in each layer.
		Ends with a (1, 1) convolution layer with 1 filter.
